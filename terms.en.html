2. Description of the Service

Reclaim is a digital wellbeing and personal productivity application. It allows users to:

  • Temporarily block access to distracting apps through various configurable modes (time slots, usage limits, geofencing, Pomodoro sessions, scheduled access).
  • View aggregated statistics on device usage, distractions avoided, minutes recovered.
  • Maintain a personal wellbeing diary (optional).
  • Configure low-level operating system permissions to enable blocking (Accessibility Service on Android, Family Controls on iOS).

Reclaim is not a medical device, does not replace professional assistance for behavioral addictions or psychological disorders, and does not provide health advice. It is a personal time management tool.
